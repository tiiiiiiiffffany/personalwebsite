---The first international official student forum in Taiwan that assembles scholars from different fields, leaders from business enterprises and student delegates from every corner of the world.---

  Established in 2008, Global Initiatives Symposium is the first international official student forum in Taiwan. Pronounced by its abbreviation, GIS hopes to gather young adults worldwide to discuss international issues nowadays and to devise possible essays or action projects that can promote local development.
   GIS Taiwan holds the largest student forum in July annually. Through assembling scholars from different fields, leaders from business enterprises and student delegates from every corner of the world, GIS Taiwan integrates the application of academy and practice. It’s meant to provide an interdisciplinary and cross-generation platform for the goal to produce a practical action project.
